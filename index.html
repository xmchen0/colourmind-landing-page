The Mind of
a Child Living with Autism


A self-empowering educational, communication tool to help children living with autism to express emotions when feelings have not been able to run their course.

Recognising human emotions can be tricky. However, breaking the chain into small steps allows kids to build self-regulation skills in manageable increments.
